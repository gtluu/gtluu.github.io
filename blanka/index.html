BLANKA is a tool developed in Python 2 to perform noise and blank subtraction on mass spectrometry data sets. It is currently able to take a variety of proprietary and open source data formats as input and outputs data in .mzXML format.


Download BLANKA here.

If you use BLANKA, please cite:

Jessica L. Cleary, Gordon T. Luu, Emily C. Pierce, Rachel J. Dutton, Laura M. Sanchez (2019). BLANKA: an Algorithm for Blank Subtraction in Mass Spectrometry of Complex Biological Samples. Journal of The American Society for Mass Spectrometry. 30(8): 1426-1434. doi: 10.1007/s13361-019-02185-8.
